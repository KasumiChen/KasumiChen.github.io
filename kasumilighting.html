Kasumi Lighting

Introduction

Kasumi Lighting is a Stylized Render Pipeline for Unity that I made in my free time.
It is designed to support a variety of styles, including Manga, American Comics, Watercolor ... with a large range of custom effects..


Core Features

Stylized Lighting Model
  • Custom Ray Diffusion Model and Color Model
  • Stylized Metallics, smoothness, Clear Coat and BRDF
  • Ambient Occulusion and Global Illumination
Traditional Workflow Support
  • Alpha map, normal map, specular map...
  • Transparency and Alpha cutout, culling
  • Layer Blending
  • Vertex Animation
Stylized Effects
  • Custom Object Outline
  • Custom Shadow
  • Autofill surface with grass/snow/sand..
  • Color Interpolation
Additional Components
  • Stylized Water
  • Depth Fog
  • Two Directional Gaussian Blur Post Processing
