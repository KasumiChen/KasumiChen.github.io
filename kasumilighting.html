Kasumi Lighting

Kasumi Lighting is a Stylized Render Pipeline for Unity that I made in my free time. The video below demostrates some of the features.
**This is already a legacy-ed version. I'm working on a significant upgrade to make it support almost all features that a modern render pipeline does. Including normal/displacement/.../ maps, (Stylized) metallic and specular brdfs, full GI and ray traing... There will also be minor feature update such as procedural stylized sky box, edge detection and so on...
